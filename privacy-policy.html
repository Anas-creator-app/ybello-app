Ybello ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Ybello mobile application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our app. By using Ybello, you agree to this policy.

01 Information We Collect

Information you provide directly

  • Phone number (used for authentication via OTP)
  • Nickname (display name visible to other users)
  • Gender (used to match users appropriately)
  • Role preference (Host or Connect)
  • Profile tags and language preferences

Identity verification (Hosts only)

  • Government-issued ID (Aadhaar, PAN, or equivalent)
  • Selfie photograph for identity verification
  • Bank account or UPI ID for payouts
  • PAN card for tax compliance above ₹50,000/year

Information collected automatically

  • Device identifier and model information
  • IP address at login for fraud prevention
  • FCM token for push notifications
  • Call session duration and timing (server-side timestamps)
  • Coin transaction history
  • App usage patterns and feature interactions

Information we do NOT collect

  • Voice call audio is never recorded or stored
  • We do not access your contacts or location
  • We do not collect camera or video data

02 How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Authenticate you securely via phone OTP
  • Create and manage your Ybello account
  • Connect callers with hosts for voice calls
  • Process coin purchases and host earnings
  • Send call notifications via Firebase Cloud Messaging
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and policy violations
  • Calculate tier rankings and weekly bonuses for hosts
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with applicable laws and regulations

03 Voice Calls & Audio

🎙️ Ybello is a voice-only platform. All calls are real-time and are not recorded, stored, or monitored by us.

Voice calls are routed through LiveKit, a secure WebRTC infrastructure provider. Audio streams are transmitted peer-to-peer and are not accessible to Ybello or any third party.

We record only metadata about calls:

  • Call start and end timestamps (server-side)
  • Call duration in seconds
  • Participant identifiers (UIDs only)
  • Coins charged and earnings credited

This metadata is used solely for billing purposes and is stored securely in our database.

04 Payments & Earnings

For callers (coin purchases)

Coin purchases are processed through Google Play's in-app billing system via RevenueCat. We do not store your payment card details. Transaction records are maintained for billing disputes and tax compliance.

For hosts (earnings & payouts)

Host earnings are calculated server-side and stored in your Ybello wallet. Payouts are processed via Razorpay to your verified bank account or UPI ID. We maintain payout records for regulatory compliance.

⚠️ Earnings above ₹50,000 per financial year are subject to Indian income tax regulations. We may collect PAN details and issue TDS certificates as required by law.

05 KYC & Identity Verification

Hosts must complete identity verification before receiving their first payout. This process collects:

  • Government photo ID (front and back)
  • Live selfie for face matching
  • PAN card (for earnings above tax threshold)

KYC documents are stored in secure, encrypted cloud storage with restricted access. They are accessible only to authorized Ybello personnel for verification purposes and are never shared with third parties except as required by law.

KYC data is retained for a minimum of 5 years as required by Indian financial regulations.

06 Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in the following limited circumstances:

Service providers

  • Firebase (Google) — authentication, database, cloud functions, notifications
  • LiveKit — real-time voice call infrastructure
  • RevenueCat — in-app purchase management
  • Razorpay — payout processing for hosts

Legal requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Ybello, our users, or the public.

Business transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of that transaction. You will be notified via the app of any such change.

07 Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted over TLS/SSL encryption
  • Firestore security rules prevent unauthorized client access to financial fields
  • Financial operations (billing, payouts) processed exclusively server-side
  • API keys and secrets stored in Google Secret Manager
  • Firebase App Check enforced on all API endpoints
  • Device fingerprinting for fraud detection

However, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your data, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

08 Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active
  • Call session metadata is retained for 2 years for billing records
  • Transaction records are retained for 7 years for tax compliance
  • KYC documents are retained for 5 years as required by law
  • Upon account deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days except where retention is legally required

09 Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction — update inaccurate information in your profile
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and personal data
  • Portability — request your data in a machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to processing of your data in certain circumstances

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@ybello.com. We will respond within 30 days.

10 Children's Privacy

⚠️ Ybello is strictly for users aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we discover that a user is under 18, their account will be immediately terminated and their data deleted.

If you believe a minor has registered on our platform, please contact us immediately at privacy@ybello.com.
